Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a grassroots gathering of tech enthusiasts in Silicon Valley. Over the years, it has grown into a global event, welcoming thousands of participants from all around the world to discuss and showcase the latest in technology and innovation.

Our Mission

The mission of TechCon is to inspire and empower individuals in the tech community through education, collaboration, and the sharing of innovative ideas. We aim to foster diversity, creativity, and thought leadership at every level of the technology ecosystem.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Jane Li

Dr. Li is a pioneer in artificial intelligence research and has led development at major tech firms. She spoke at TechCon 2021 on the future of human-AI interaction.

Michael Arora

Founder of one of the fastest-growing fintech startups, Michael shared insights on startup culture and digital transformation at TechCon 2022.

Elena García

Elena is a robotics expert who introduced her latest work on autonomous systems and their impact on global logistics at TechCon 2023.
